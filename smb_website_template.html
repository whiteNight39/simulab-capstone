About SimuLab

Making science accessible, one simulation at a time

Built for the curious. Designed for the future.

SimuLab is a backend-first platform for simulating physics and engineering experiments. Whether you're a student, educator, or developer, SimuLab provides fast, accurate, and accessible results. No lab equipment required.

Our goal is to make hands-on science possible anywhere. With a growing library of pre-built simulations and a powerful API interface, SimuLab simplifies learning and experimentation in the classroom, on the web, or through your own apps.

How It Works

SimuLab is designed to make science simulations simple and accessible in just a few steps.

1. Choose a Simulation

Browse our growing library of experiments across physics and engineering topics.

2. Enter Parameters

Input variables like mass, voltage, or pressure depending on the experiment you selected.

3. Run the Simulation

SimuLab performs the calculations instantly and returns accurate results.

4. Analyze Results

Interpret the data, understand the output, and deepen your scientific insight.
